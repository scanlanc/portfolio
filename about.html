Paige and Lily, based in New York and London, are young, talented writers seeking opportunities to help small fine art galleries and artists studios build out their website and social media content through research and writing. As online offerings become more prominent in the art world space, written and visual material to bolster beautifully executed work can elevate the buyer experience and deal-making process.

Paige graduated from Vanderbilt University with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Art History and Corportate Strategy. She developed a passion for the art world at an early age, growing up visiting New York museums with her family and friends. Paige fostered her love for writing during her undergraduate studies and further refined her skills in professional roles with Christie's and Cavalier Galleries. At the aforementioned auction house and gallery she developed experience researching and writing for artist biographies, private sale proprosals, and catalogue notes. She currently works at David Zwirner gallery assisting the sales team. Paige is based in SoHo, New York.

Lily graduated with a Bachelor of Arts from Britain's top English Literature program at Durham University in 2018. Throughout her life, Lily has had a passion for writing and the arts, exploring these sills as a playwright, comedian, and essayist. Professionally, Lily has further expanded her skills working at the Victoria and Albert Museum in London as a Researcher in the Theatre and Performance Department. There she has assisted in the composition and editing of exhibition copt and social media posts. Additionally, Lily has been working with the renowned filmmaker and artist, Eduardo Costantine Jr., founder of Mubi and KABINETT, on developing his Instagram and website, writing captions and artist profiles. Lily is based in Southwest London.
